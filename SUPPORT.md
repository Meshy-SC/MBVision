# MBVision — Support

Thank you for using **MBVision** on Apple Vision Pro.

MBVision delivers immersive 3D workflows, live machine condition‑monitoring
data, and an AI assistant for industrial maintenance, training, and inspection.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I sign in?

MBVision uses your **iCloud account** to sync workflows and assets across
your devices. Sign in to iCloud on your Apple Vision Pro
(Settings → Apple Account) and make sure **iCloud Drive** is enabled. No
separate account is required.

### Why doesn't my content appear?

- Confirm you are signed in to iCloud and connected to the internet.
- Pull down on the library screen to refresh, or relaunch the app.
- Content authored on another device may take a few minutes to propagate
  through iCloud.

### How do I place 3D content in my room?

After opening a workflow:

1. Look at a flat surface (floor or table).
2. Tap the **placement indicator** that appears in the immersive space.
3. The 3D model anchors to that location. Walk around it freely.

Make sure your room has adequate lighting and that hand tracking is enabled
in visionOS settings.

### The microphone or voice assistant isn't working

The in-app AI assistant uses your microphone for voice questions.

- Check that you granted microphone permission to MBVision
  (Settings → Apps → MBVision → Microphone).
- If you prefer to type questions, use the text field instead — voice is
  optional.

### How do I pair my iPhone companion?

The optional iPhone companion uses your **local Wi-Fi network** to mirror
step navigation.

1. Make sure your iPhone and Apple Vision Pro are on the **same Wi-Fi**.
2. Open the companion app on iPhone and the main MBVision app on Apple
   Vision Pro.
3. Tap the iPhone icon in the toolbar and accept the pairing prompt.
4. Allow **Local Network** permission when asked.

### Can I use the app offline?

Yes — once a workflow has been opened, its 3D assets and steps are cached
on-device so you can review them without a network connection. Live machine
data and the AI assistant require an internet connection.

### How do I reset the app?

To clear locally cached content, go to **Settings → Apps → MBVision** on
your Apple Vision Pro and use **Reset**. Your iCloud-stored workflows are
not affected.

## System requirements

- Apple Vision Pro running **visionOS 26 or later**
- An active iCloud account
- An internet connection for the initial download and for live data /
  AI assistant features
